Aaron has a B.S. in Engineering with an Electrical specialty from the Colorado School of Mines. He further focused his skillset in business management by earning a Masters of Business Administration (M.B.A.) From Mesa State College.

His responsibilities at WestWater include coordinating new projects with clients including budget and schedule estimates, assigning project managers, and scheduling work. This includes management of WestWater’s GSA Schedule. He provides project management, coordination and technical services for environmental analyses and permitting of mineral and land proposals. Aaron assists project managers in keeping all projects on budget and prepares invoices for completed work.

Aaron is also responsible for the GIS department at WestWater, which develops project specific graphics and maps, detailing environmental impact and mitigations and provides project managers with spatial and data analysis of project specific field data. Finally, he manages, maintains and upgrades the company computer and network systems.
