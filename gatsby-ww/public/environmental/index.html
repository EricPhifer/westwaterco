Our Environmental Consultants

We maintain close working relationships with Federal, State and local regulatory agencies including the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), U.S. Forest Service (USFS), U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (USFWS), Army Corps of Engineers (ACOE), and Colorado Parks and Wildlife (CPW).

Many of our team members are former agency representatives from Western Colorado, providing WestWater with a unique perspective and ability to foresee and avoid potential project issues while focusing on client and project needs.

WestWater has provided services related to oil and gas, oil shale, nahcolite, coal, uranium exploration and development, agency planning and resource enhancement, pipeline and power line projects, road and highway improvement projects, recreational trail projects, municipal water and sanitary sewer projects.
